An Antidote to Endless Negativity

Research shows that when just 3½% of people actively participate in sustained nonviolent movements, those movements succeed. We're documenting the proof points that this critical mass is already building—in institutions, organizations, and communities of every kind.

Think of it as evidence that ordinary people in every kind of role are creating extraordinary progress. From legal victories protecting fundamental rights to faith communities adapting to serve the most vulnerable, from public servants finding ways to help to neighbors organizing solutions.

This isn't just feel-good journalism. It's strategic documentation of the momentum that's already happening, creating an antidote to negativity and proof that change is closer than it feels.
